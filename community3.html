community engagements


Digital literacy and cybersecurity awawarness sessions

After successfully completing my Global UGRAD Semester Exchange Program in the US and immersing myself in a comprehensive cybersecurity course, I experienced a profound awakening to the paramount significance of cybersecurity in our 21st-century world. This transformative realization not only inspired me but also compelled me to take decisive action. Fueled by this newfound passion, I committed myself to conducting impactful awareness sessions, with a special focus on underprivileged students in Pakistan, particularly girls. To date, I am immensely proud to have equipped over 1,000 students with crucial digital literacy and cybersecurity knowledge. This accomplishment stands as a testament to the positive change we can bring to our communities. It wouldn't have been possible without the unwavering support and guidance from Global UGRAD, the U.S. Department of State, and IREX. Their belief in this initiative has not only amplified its impact but also turned my vision into a powerful, tangible reality, for which I am eternally grateful.
