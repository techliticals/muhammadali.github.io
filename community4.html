community engagements


Child Protection and Inclusion of Excluded in Society

My commitment to volunteer work extended to raising awareness on critical social issues, specifically child protection and transgender rights. I dedicated my time to conducting awareness sessions, offering my experiences and insights to those who needed it the most.
In child protection awareness sessions, I aimed to equip both children and their guardians with the knowledge and tools necessary to ensure their safety and well-being. It was not just about sharing information; it was about empowering individuals to protect themselves and their loved ones.
Similarly, my involvement in transgender issues was driven by a deep belief in the importance of equal rights and improved standards of living for all members of our society. I engaged with transgender individuals, listening to their experiences and challenges, and providing ideas on how they could achieve financial independence and lead fulfilling lives. It was about recognizing their potential, the opportunities available to them, and supporting their journey towards a brighter future.
